HOW TO PLAY

🚀 Control your rocket through dangerous space corridors!

☄️ Dodge asteroids and planets — they move, bounce, and orbit!

🛡️ You have 3 Energy Shields. Each collision destroys one shield. No shields = Game Over!

🏁 Complete the distance bar to clear each level.

⭐ Earn up to 3 Stars per level based on shields remaining and time!

Controls: WASD / Arrow Keys to move, or move your mouse over the canvas.

Pause: Press P or Escape
